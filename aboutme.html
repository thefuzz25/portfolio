About Me


‘Not all those who Wander are Lost.’

Faiz Zaidi is an college student and amateur artist who’s work flirts with both sketches and words. When not behind a desk, he’s an avid reader and athlete.

He was born in Dehradun, in 2005 and has since revolved around the Sun 18 times. Being the son of an Olive Green, he has wheels on his feet and his home is always spinning around. As such, he has resided in 9 cities so far, attended 10+ schools and many more ¡welcomes and goodbyes! than necessary…

And at the time of writing (2023), he's a first year undergrad at IIT Kharagpur. Currently pursuing bachelors in Manufacturing Science and Enggineering, but also with a bit of coding and all things creative.

Fun fact: He does love rain per se but rather from the inside of the house and a glass window to have a view of it with a cup of hot coffee in his hands.
